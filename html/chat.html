    It seems obvious once you know about it, but you're not limited to uploading previously taken photos in Instagram. There's a quick way to snap and upload a photo from within the app itself!

    Hi - I'm an AT&T wireless expert. It looks like you're on a Galaxy S9. I am too and I've seen this issue before. It typically happens because you rejected Instagram's request to access the camera the first time it was launched.

    Go to your phone settings and click on Applications > Instagram. Then make sure that Camera Access is enabled in the Permissions. You may need to quit the Instagram app and restart it, but it should work after that.
